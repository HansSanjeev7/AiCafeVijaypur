This Cookie Policy explains how Ai Cafe, Vijaypur ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. This policy is designed to help you understand what cookies are, how we use them, and the choices you have regarding their use.

By continuing to browse or use our website, you agree to our use of cookies as described in this Cookie Policy and our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer, tablet, mobile phone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and give website owners information about how their sites are used.

Cookies may be either "persistent" cookies or "session" cookies:

  • Persistent cookies remain on your device even after you close your browser, and will be activated again when you visit the website that created the cookie. They will remain until they expire or until you delete them.
  • Session cookies are temporary and are deleted from your device when you close your browser.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use different types of cookies for different purposes. These include:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

Preference Cookies

These cookies enable the website to remember information that changes the way the website behaves or looks, such as your preferred language or the region you are in.

Statistics/Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve the way our website works.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to track visitors across websites. The intention is to display ads that are relevant and engaging for the individual user.
