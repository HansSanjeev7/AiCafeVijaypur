What is Google Business Management?

Google Business Management is the process of setting up, optimizing, and maintaining your Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business). This ensures that your business appears correctly in Google Search and Google Maps, helping customers find you quickly, trust your brand, and engage with your services.
